Design Notes
Hero: Total impressions across ALL channels — organic social, paid ads, and search combined. The single biggest number representing total brand exposure.

Stacked bar: 32px tall, three colored segments showing proportional contribution of each channel type. Percentages labeled inside each segment. The visual immediately shows the balance — is the brand earning attention, buying it, or being found through search?

Channel rows: Tight, compact rows directly below the bar they describe. Each row shows the channel color dot, name, raw impression count, and directional change. No extra padding — these label the bar, they don't compete with it.

Distinct from Brand Pulse: This card has no score, no conceptual layers, no modeling. It's the factual channel-by-channel distribution. Brand Pulse answers "how healthy is your brand visibility?" while this answers "where is your brand showing up?"

Channel composition adapts: If a client has no paid ads, the bar shows only organic and search segments. The rows flex accordingly.

Lives on both dashboards.
