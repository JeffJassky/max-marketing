Design Notes
Title: "Marketing Efficiency Ratio" — clear, descriptive, no jargon abbreviations.

Badge: "Efficiency Ratio" labels what the number is, not a judgment about what it means.

Hero number: Shows the ratio as-is. When spend exceeds revenue, the number goes negative (e.g., -0.4x).

Combined bar: Single bar where the indigo portion represents ad spend within the full revenue bar. The proportional relationship is immediately visible. "Revenue vs. Ad Spend" title is bold and readable — not the light uppercase treatment.

Sparkline: Trend over the selected period with a 1.0x threshold line for context.

Attribution: Stays as a quiet footnote at the bottom — context, not headline.
