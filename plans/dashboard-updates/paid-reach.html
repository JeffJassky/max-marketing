Design Notes
Hero: Total paid impressions as the headline number. Reach and total spend shown as compact supporting stats so the user sees volume, unique people, and cost in one glance.

Efficiency section: "Cost Per 1,000 Impressions" is spelled out rather than "CPM" — clarity over jargon. Ordered most efficient first so the user immediately sees where their money goes furthest. "Best" badge highlights the winner.

Paid vs. Organic bar: A single compact proportional bar connecting this card's story to the broader Brand Pulse. Tells the user whether they're earning attention or buying it, without duplicating Brand Pulse's full breakdown.

Adapts to platforms: Only shows rows for connected ad platforms. One platform = one row. The "Best" badge only appears when there are 2+ platforms to compare.
