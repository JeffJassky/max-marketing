Design Notes
CTR placement: Positioned right-aligned above the impressions-to-clicks bar, inline with the section title. No separate row — keeps the card compact.

Hero: Total search impressions (all queries) as the big number. Momentum badge gives directional health at a glance.

Bar 1: Branded vs. non-branded split. Shows what proportion of search visibility comes from people who already know the brand name vs. discovery searches.

Bar 2: Impressions-to-clicks funnel. The filled cyan portion represents clicks within total impressions. CTR and its change indicator sit above the bar for quick reference.
