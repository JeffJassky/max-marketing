Design Notes
Hero: Total blended follower count as a single large number. Gives users the "big picture" answer to "how big is my audience?" before breaking it down.

Sparkline: Shows the blended total over time. Follower counts tend to climb steadily (or plateau), so this line will typically look smoother than conversion sparklines.

By Platform: Ordered largest to smallest. Platform-colored dots for quick scanning. Each row shows the count and its period change. Only connected platforms appear — 2 platforms means 2 rows, 4 means 4. The card height flexes naturally.

Fastest growing callout: Highlights the platform with the strongest growth rate — gives the user one actionable takeaway. If all platforms are declining, this row could adapt to show "Least decline" or simply not appear.

Lives on both dashboards. Same card, same data, same treatment regardless of Shopify or Brand dashboard.
